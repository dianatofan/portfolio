Hi again! I'm Diana Tofan.

I'm a frontend developer based in Copenhagen passionate about UX design. I would love to change that and step into the world of UX/UI! Having a computer science background, I can easily identify the technical limitations when prototyping a solution since I understand both sides of the coin. This helps me come up with feasible designs that are possible to implement by engineers in a minimum of time.

Constantly improving user interfaces is the thing I find most rewarding in the web development world.
